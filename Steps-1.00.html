Executive Summary 1

This Power BI dashboard delivers a data-driven approach to enhance the bus safety across London boroughs, enabling Transport for London (TFL) to proactively identify high-risk boroughs, identify the service provider operators with maximum number of incidents, and implement targeted interventions by initiating Vision Zero Mission. The dashboard provides key insights into borough-specific trends, showcasing the impact of safety measures on reducing injuries and financial costs by analyzing incidents from 2015 to 2018. Additionally, it enhances transparency and accountability by monitoring operator performance and ensuring compliance with safety standards. With 53% of London’s trips relying on TFL bus services, this initiative strengthens urban transit safety, operational efficiency, and cost-effectiveness, reinforcing TFL’s commitment to a safer and smarter public transport network. 



Background:

Public transportation is the backbone of urban mobility in London, yet passenger safety remains a pressing concern. In spite of establishing safety protocols, some routes and boroughs exhibit higher accident and injury rates, contributing to increased hospitalization and financial strain. Without advanced data analysis, TFL lacks the ability to proactively identify high-risk areas and take preventive measures. The dataset provides a comprehensive overview of bus incidents categorized by  operators, boroughs, and injury result description. It allows for a structured examination of transit safety. By leveraging this dataset, authorities can gain valuable insights to prioritize resources and implement targeted interventions for risk mitigation.




Objective:

This report presents an analytical deep dive into bus incidents across London Boroughs, with a particular focus on key performance indicators such as Total incidents, Fatal rate, Injury rate, and Hospitalization rate, along with year-over-year trends.  The analysis provides a critical perspective on how different Group Names and Operators impact urban transit safety across boroughs.   The primary reason for developing this report to identify high risk borough for implementing Vision Zero strategy.  It will enhance urban transit safety by leveraging data-driven insights to identify trends, mitigate risks of hospitalization rates, injury severity. As an outcome, savings in healthcare expenses can reduce the financial , social and operational burden.





Problem Statement:  

Regardless of the extensive use of public transportation in London, bus-related incidents continue to pose significant safety risks, financial burdens, and operational challenges. High hospitalization rates, preventable injuries, and recurring accidents highlight the urgent need for proactive safety measures.
