KPI- Current Year Incidents 

Total Incidents- Click on three dots of the TFL Bus Safety v.2 Table > New Measure > In the formula bar, write the following DAX to create Measure-Current Year Incidents. 
 
Current Year Incidents = 
VAR SelectedYear = MAX('CalendarTable'[Year]) 
RETURN 
   CALCULATE ( 
        COUNT ('TFL Bus Safety v.2'[Date Of Incident]), 
        'CalendarTable'[Year] = SelectedYear 
    ) 
  1.  Click on Visualizations on the right side of the Report > Build visuals > Click on Card > Select Measure “Current Year Incidents” from Data > Format your visuals > Callout value Font- Arial 20 > Color #6B91C9 > General > Properties > Size- Height 100, Width 189 > Position – Horizontal 3, Vertical 189 > Padding – Top 20px, Bottom 0px, Both sides 5px. > Title- Off > > Effect Transparency 95% > Visual Border- On, Color #CCCCCC, Rounded corner 0px, Width 1px.

Sub KPI – YoY% - 
.LY Total Incidents = 
VAR SelectedYear =MAX('CalendarTable'[Year]) 
RETURN 
  COALESCE( CALCULATE( 
        COUNT('TFL Bus Safety v.2'[Date Of Incident]), 
        'CalendarTable'[Year] = SelectedYear - 1 
    ),0) 
 
Incidents YoY% = 
 COALESCE(DIVIDE(([Current Year Incidents]-[.LY Total Incidents]),[.LY Total Incidents]),0) 
 
  1. Click on Visualizations on the right side of the Report > Build visuals > Click on Card > Select Measure “YoY” from the Data > Format your visuals > Callout value Font- Arial 12 > Color #252423 > General > Properties > Size- Height 30, Width 72 > Position – Horizontal 120, Vertical 258 > Padding – Top 5px, Bottom 5px, Left side 10px, Right side 0px. > Title- Off > > Effect Transparency 95% > Visual Border-Off.
