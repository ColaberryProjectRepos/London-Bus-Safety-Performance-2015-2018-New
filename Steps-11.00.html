Importing data to Power BI 11

Click on the red button to upload the dataset.



  1. Download the data from the data source. The link is given below.
  2. https://data.world/makeovermonday/2018w51
  3.  Download the data to your machine, rename and save it in a folder. This data set is in Excel format.
  4. Open Power Bi desktop. Click on the blank report, then click on Get data from the top Menu bar. Select Excel data.
  5. Select the Sheet from the Navigate pane and then click on Transform data.
  6. The data type of “Route” column is Whole number, though it has text data.
  7. Right click on the column Rout, choose the datatype as Text and then Apply and Close.
  8. In the Borough column, right click> Replace value> from & To and. Left top, click on Apply and close.
  9. Rename the Sheet-1 as TFL Bus Safety v.2
  10. Change the date format to mm/dd/yyyy in “Date Of Incident” column.
