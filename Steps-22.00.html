Incidents by Fatal Rate (Scattered Chart)



  1. Click on Visualizations > Build Visualizations >Scatter chart > Values>Drag “Borough” from TFL Bus Safety v.2 to  Value > Drag “Injury Rate%” from TFL Bus Safety v.2 to X-axis > Drag  Hospitalization Rate % from TFL Bus Safety v.2 to y-axis > Drag “Current Year Incidents” from TFL Bus Safety v.2 to Size > > Drag “Fatal Rate%” from TFL Bus Safety v.2 to Tooltip.
  2. Click on Format your visualization > Keep all the range in Auto > Values Arial 9, color #666666 > Title On > Marker Size multiplier -11 > Color > fx 
  3. Effect > Transparency 95%, Border On. Copy the heading bar from Incidents Chart and paste it on the heading of the Scattered Chart. Format the Title to Incidents by Fata Rate.
