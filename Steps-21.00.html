 Injury by Incident Type (Clustered Bar Chart)




  1. Creating “Treated on Scene%”, “Minor Injury%”, “Serious Injury%” Measures.
  2. Treated on Scene% =
  3.  COALESCE(DIVIDE('TFL Bus Safety v.2'[.Treated_on_Scene],'TFL Bus Safety v.2'[Current Year Incidents]),0)
  4. Minor Injury% =
  5.  COALESCE(DIVIDE('TFL Bus Safety v.2'[.Minor_Injuries],'TFL Bus Safety v.2'[Current Year Incidents]),0)
  6. Serious Injury% = 
  7. COALESCE(DIVIDE('TFL Bus Safety v.2'[.Serious_Injuries],'TFL Bus Safety v.2'[Current Year Incidents]),0) 
  8. Click on Visualizations > Build Visualizations > Clustered Bar Chart > Y-axis >Drag “Years” from CalendarTable to x-axis > Drag “Treated on Scene%”, “Minor Injury%”, “Serious Injury%” from TFL BusSafety v.2 to Line y-axis.
