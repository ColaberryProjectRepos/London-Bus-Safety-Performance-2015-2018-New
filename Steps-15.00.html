Adding Year Filter as a Slider and Formatting 15




  1. Click on the Visualization pane > Slicer > Data on the right Side
  2. Click the drop-down arrow on CalendarTable. Select “Sum of Year” column to add to the Slicer.
  3. Click on the Visualizations > Format your Visuals > Visuals > Slicer settings > Style- Click drop down and choose Between > Slicer Header- Off > Values – Font Arial 9, Font color #0856F2 > Slider- On > Color #666666
  4. Click on General > Properties > Size- Height 98, Width 189 > Position – Horizontal 3, Vertical-90 > Padding – Top 20px, Bottom 10px, Right & Left sides 5px > Effect- Background- Color #FFFFFF, Transparency 95% > Visual Border- On, Color #CCCCCC, Rounded Corner 0px, Width 1px 



Formatting the Year Filter 
  1. Click on Insert from the top Menu bar > Text box
  2. Click on the Format text box > General > Properties > Size- Height 21, Width 189 > Position – Horizontal 3, Vertical 90 > Padding – Top 3px, Bottom 5px, Right & Left sides 5px. > Title- On > Text- Year of Incident, Font- Arial 11 Bold, Text color #0856F2, Horizontal alignment- Left > Effect > Background – Color #DED2A6, Transparency 0% > Visual Border- Off
